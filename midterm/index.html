Life of a Starfish

Starfish live in oceans around the world. They move slowly and use their arms to catch food. They can live in shallow water and deep sea environments. Starfish are known for their ability to regenerate lost arms.

Facts About Starfish

  • Starfish are marine animals
  • They usually have five arms
  • They can regenerate lost limbs
  • They live on the ocean floor
